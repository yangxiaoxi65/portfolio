Allara Health Prototype

Team Work by Xiaoxi Yang, Yui Musha, Lanxi Li, Shiyu Yan

Project Overview

This project aimed to redesign and improve the website onboarding flow of Allara Health, a virtual care provider specializing in women's hormonal and metabolic health. Our team created a new interactive prototype focused on enhancing user clarity, personalization, and trust.

Context and Problem Statement

Allara Health's original website presented challenges for potential patients, leaving them uncertain about its suitability. Specific issues included:

  • Lack of clear messaging about Allara's services
  • Unfamiliarity with Allara's offerings
  • Patients' skepticism of doctors

I took the lead in gathering initial research and worked on identifying these core issues by analyzing user behavior and feedback.

Research and Project Goals

The goal of this project was to redesign the Allara website to address key issues faced by potential patients. These issues included uncertainty about Allara's services, lack of brand awareness, and skepticism towards healthcare providers. The redesign aimed to clarify Allara's offerings, build trust with patients, and effectively communicate the benefits of choosing Allara for their healthcare needs.

  • Improve clarity about Allara's unique services and suitability for patients
  • Build trust with patients by showcasing the doctors and personalizing their experience
  • Simplify the onboarding process to reduce friction

I helped refine the project goals by communicating with the client to clarify patient onboarding requirements and potential pitfalls in the original flow.

Solution - Interactive Prototype

We addressed the identified issues through a series of iterations, refining our solution to produce an intuitive and effective onboarding flow.

Learnings and Outcome

  • Prioritizing patient engagement led to a more inviting onboarding experience
  • Breaking down long onboarding forms into digestible steps reduced user friction
  • Collaborating closely with stakeholders ensured that all feedback was accounted for

I learned the importance of balancing client feedback with user needs, refining designs iteratively, and ensuring navigation remains intuitive throughout the onboarding journey.
